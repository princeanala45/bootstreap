Facilitys Of our Grounds

  • The stadium is built on 63 acres of land and has four entry points, with a metro line at one of the entry points.
  • It has 76 corporate boxes that can hold 25 persons each, a 55-room clubhouse, an Olympic size swimming pool, and four dressing rooms.
  • A food court and hospitality area are available in each spectator stand.
  • Seating: The main seating structure and tiered seating for spectators
  • Locker rooms: Changing rooms for teams and officials with showers, toilets, and rest areas
  • Hospitality areas: Areas with premium experiences for VIPs and VVIPs
  • Restrooms: there are 24 restrooms in the stadium
